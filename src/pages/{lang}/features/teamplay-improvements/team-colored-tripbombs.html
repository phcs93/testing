Tripbombs laserlines will be colored according to the team that has placed them. You can enable this feature in the menu 
Netplay Options > NetFlags > [TEAMS] Colored Tripbombs
. Note that, if enabled, map spawned tripbomb laserlines will be gray colored, to avoid confusion. O laser das tripbombs terá a cor do time que as colocou. Você pode habilitar isso no menu 
Netplay Options > NetFlags > [TEAMS] Colored Tripbombs
. Note que, se habilitado, as tripbombs originarias do mapa terão seus lasers na cor cinza, para evitar confusões.
